Teaching

I developed the curriculum for a new course Statistical Methods for Data Science (STAT131A) with Professor Adityanand Guntuboyina and Professor Elizabeth Purdom in Fall 2016. STAT131A is an upper-division course offered by the Statistics Department at UC Berkeley. It covers a broad range of topics from parametric and non-parametric statistical tests to regression and classification models. I served as a graduate student instructor for the course in Spring 2017.
